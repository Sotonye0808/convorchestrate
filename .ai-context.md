## What It Does

Convorchestrate is a config-driven, multi-tenant WhatsApp conversation orchestration SaaS. It executes workflows defined in JSON and avoids hardcoded business rules. The system automates contact verification campaigns, runs marketing engagement funnels, and mediates buyer-seller conversations in marketplace contexts.

## Architecture Principles

- **Config-driven first**: no hardcoded workflow logic
- **Adapter isolation**: core never imports adapters
- **Tenant-first**: every query filters by tenant_id
- **All messages through BullMQ**: never inline on webhook thread
